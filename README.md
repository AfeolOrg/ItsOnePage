# ItsOnePage

## Own your website.
### Pay only for your domain.

**Open-source one-page websites without subscriptions, tracking, or vendor lock-in.**

Build fast, clean, privacy-friendly one-page websites that you fully own and can deploy anywhere.

---

## Why?

Most website builders don't let you truly own your website.

They own the platform.

They control your content.

They decide the pricing.

ItsOnePage takes a different approach.

- Own your files.
- Own your domain.
- Choose your hosting.
- Deploy on free hosting platforms or your own server.
- No subscriptions.
- No ads.
- No tracking.
- No vendor lock-in.

The only thing you should pay for is **your domain**.

Everything else should be your choice.
